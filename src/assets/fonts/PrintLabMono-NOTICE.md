# PrintLab Mono

PrintLab Mono is a derivative work of **Bitstream Vera Sans Mono**
(Copyright (c) 2003 by Bitstream, Inc.), used as the canvas substitute for
ZPL device fonts A/C/D/F/G.

## Modifications

Relative to upstream Bitstream Vera Sans Mono:

- The dash glyphs (hyphen, en-dash, em-dash) are replaced with a wider,
  taller bar matching Zebra's device-font dash (the upstream hyphen is too
  narrow/short for ZPL fidelity). All other glyphs and metrics are unchanged.
- Renamed from "Bitstream Vera Sans Mono" to "PrintLab Mono" as the license
  requires for modified fonts. The original copyright, trademark and
  permission notices are retained in the font's `name` table and below.
